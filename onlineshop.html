About Ozo

Ozo is an Indian luxury fashion house founded in 1910 by Dkg in Delhi. It is privately owned by the Goel's and has been headquartered in London since 2018.Ozo specializes in women's ready-to-wear, luxurious, and classic colognes and scents. Ozo is well known for its No. 5 perfume and "Ozo Suit".
